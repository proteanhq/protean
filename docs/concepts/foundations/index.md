approach to modeling software.

- [**Bounded Contexts**](./bounded-contexts.md) — Explicit boundaries around domain models.
- [**Analysis Model**](./analysis-model.md) — Translating domain knowledge into code.
- [**Identity**](./identity.md) — What makes an entity unique.
- [**Invariants**](./invariants.md) — Business rules that must always hold true.
- [**Changing State**](./changing-state.md) — How state transitions work in a domain.
- [**Streams**](./streams.md) — Event streams and their role in the architecture.
