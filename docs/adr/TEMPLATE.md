## Context

What forces are at play? What problem needed solving? What constraints or requirements
shaped the decision? Write in prose — explain the situation as you would to a new team
member who has domain context but wasn't present for the discussion.

## Decision

What we chose, stated in active voice. "We will use X" or "We chose Y because Z."
Be specific enough that someone could implement or verify this decision from the
description alone.

## Consequences

What becomes easier or harder as a result of this decision. Include both positive
and negative consequences — every decision has trade-offs. State them plainly without
editorializing.

*(Optional — include when multiple viable options were evaluated.)*
