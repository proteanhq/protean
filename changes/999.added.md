Protean deprecations now flow through a single reusable mechanism, so every deprecation cites a specific removal version in a consistent format and emits a `protean.exceptions.ProteanDeprecationWarning` (a `DeprecationWarning` subclass). Scope your test config to that category to fail CI on deprecated usage regardless of where the warning is raised (pytest `filterwarnings = error::protean.exceptions.ProteanDeprecationWarning`). The `--debug` CLI flags and the `assert_valid`/`assert_invalid` testing helpers now warn through this mechanism.
