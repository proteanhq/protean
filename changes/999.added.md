Protean deprecations now flow through a single reusable mechanism, so every deprecation cites a specific removal version in a consistent format and emits a `DeprecationWarning` subclass. Any CI that promotes deprecation warnings to errors (`-W error::DeprecationWarning`, or a pytest `filterwarnings = error::DeprecationWarning` entry) therefore surfaces them regardless of where the warning is raised. The `--debug` CLI flags and the `assert_valid`/`assert_invalid` testing helpers now warn through this mechanism.
