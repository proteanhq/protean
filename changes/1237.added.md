Added lifecycle/audit field support. `DateTime` and `Date` fields gain Django-parity `auto_now_add` (stamp on create) and `auto_now` (stamp on every save) flags, and domains gain a registered pre-persist hook — `register_aggregate_enricher()` / `@domain.aggregate_enricher` — that stamps cross-cutting fields such as `created_by`/`updated_by` from the domain context just before an aggregate is saved.
