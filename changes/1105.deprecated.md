The `Method` and `Nested` serializer fields, and the `pickled` argument on `List`, are deprecated and will be removed in v1.0.0. `Method`/`Nested` supported a serializer element that no longer exists, and `List(pickled=...)` was a dead kwarg with no effect. Prefer `List(content_type=ValueObject(...))` over `ValueObjectList` at the declaration surface.
