Reflect a DB-assigned `Auto(increment=True)` identifier back onto the aggregate when persisting through a relational adapter (SQLAlchemy → Postgres/SQLite/MSSQL). After `repository.add()` (or `dao.create()`), the generated primary key now populates the original instance, including when the persist runs inside an outer `UnitOfWork`, instead of staying `None` until commit. Because reflecting the value forces the insert to flush early, a constraint violation on such an auto-increment insert may now surface at `add()`/`create()` time rather than only at commit.
