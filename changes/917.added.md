Inject OpenTelemetry `trace_id`, `span_id`, and `trace_flags` into every log record when `telemetry.enabled=True`, enabling log↔trace correlation in Grafana Tempo, Honeycomb, Datadog APM, and other APM tools. Introduces `OTelTraceContextFilter` (stdlib) and `protean_otel_processor` (structlog), wired automatically by `Domain.configure_logging()`. Correlation extraction now falls back to `g.correlation_id`/`g.causation_id` when no `g.message_in_context` is in scope — closing the "HTTP route logging before process()" and "CLI command logging with add_context" gaps.
