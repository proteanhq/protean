Added a cross-adapter conformance suite for the query-API primitives (`count()`, `only()` projection, `isnull`/`lt`/`lte` lookups, `F()` column comparison, `_delete_top`, and `_claim`), so each adapter is held to the same behaviour for the capabilities it declares and divergence fails a test rather than reaching production. The contract and its capability tiers are recorded in ADR-0023.
