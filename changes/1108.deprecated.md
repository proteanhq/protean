The `published` option on a command is deprecated and will be removed in v1.0.0. Commands are internal to the bounded context — only events participate in the published language — so the option was silently absorbed with no effect. Passing `published` to `@domain.command(...)` or `domain.register(Cmd, ...)` now surfaces a `DeprecationWarning` (`RemovedInProtean10Warning`) at command-declaration/registration time and the option is dropped; `protean check` additionally reports the usage as a `DEPRECATED_OPTION` warning diagnostic. Events are unaffected and keep the `published` option.
