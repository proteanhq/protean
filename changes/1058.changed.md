Consolidated CLI domain-loading onto the shared `protean.cli._helpers.load_domain` helper. The `dlq`, `events`, `subscriptions`, `database`, `snapshot`, and `projection` subcommands previously carried byte-identical private copies (or inline blocks) of the derive/init/abort logic; they now call the single canonical loader, so the "Error loading Protean domain: …" abort path and `init()`-before-`domain_context()` ordering stay identical across every command. No public-facing behavior changes. Subcommands with genuinely divergent semantics (`check`, `upgrade`, `generate`, `shell`, `observatory`, `hooks`, IR loading) are intentionally left untouched.
