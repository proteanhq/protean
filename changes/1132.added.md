The compatibility checker is now evolution-aware. Deprecation grace (a field deprecated at or past its removal version is a safe removal) now applies to all persisted elements, including internal and event-sourced events, not only published contracts. An event version bump whose old→new path is covered by a registered upcaster chain has its schema-transformation changes (field removals, type changes, required-field additions, the `__type__` version-string bump) downgraded from breaking to safe, citing the mitigating upcaster; upcaster chains are now projected into the IR (`upcasters` section) so the checker can consult them without a live domain.
