A `$all` (cross-category) event-store subscription no longer silently skips an event whose `global_position` commits out of order. Because `global_position` can be assigned across categories out of commit order (a lower value committing after a higher one), advancing the checkpoint to the last position seen could permanently miss the late-committing lower one — at zero apparent lag. `$all` subscriptions now hold at the first gap and process only the contiguous run, abandoning a gap that stays unfilled longer than `gap_timeout_seconds` (default 5, under `[server.event_store_subscription]`) as a rolled-back position. Single-category subscriptions are unaffected — they are already gap-free by the store's per-category commit-order guarantee.
