Clarified that `protean-check-staleness --fix` writes a **canonical** IR baseline (sorted keys, no volatile `generated_at`), byte-identical to `protean ir show --canonical`. This has been the behavior since the canonical baseline landed; the hook's `--help` and the compatibility-checking guide now say so explicitly, so projects committing canonical baselines know it is safe to keep `--fix` enabled instead of dropping it to avoid diff churn.
